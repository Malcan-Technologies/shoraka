Shariah Compliance Requirement Notice

CashSouk operates as a fully Shariah-compliant financing platform.

Before proceeding to sign-up, please note that all issuer applications are subject to a mandatory Shariah screening and financial ratio assessment as part of our onboarding and approval process.

Businesses primarily involved in non-Shariah-compliant activities including but not limited to:

  • Conventional banking or interest-based lending
  • Conventional insurance
  • Gambling
  • Liquor or liquor-related activities
  • Pork or pork-related activities
  • Non-halal food and beverages
  • Tobacco-related activities
  • Interest (riba)-based income or investments
  • Any other non-compliant activities

Businesses primarily involved in the above activities may not qualify for listing on the platform.

By continuing to the sign-up page, you acknowledge that your application will undergo Shariah screening and approval is subject to compliance requirements.
