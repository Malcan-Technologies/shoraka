## What this is

**Get Financed** is how you start a financing request in CashSouk Issuer. The first step in every application is **Financing Type**: you choose the **financing product**. **Each product defines its own steps** after that (different order, different screens, different requirements). Every workflow ends with **Declarations** before you can submit. The progress bar shows exactly what your chosen product needs.

Until you submit, the application stays **Draft**. You can leave and come back later. Open **Application** in the sidebar to see all your applications, open one, and continue where you left off.

After submit, the team reviews the request. Funding is not guaranteed.

You need a verified organization with onboarding **Completed** before **Get Financed** is available. Platform fees may apply when payment is turned on for application submission.

## Start from Get Financed

1. Open the issuer portal.
2. Click **Get Financed** on the dashboard, or open **Application** in the sidebar and click **Get Financed** there.
3. On **New Application**, read the product descriptions and select the product that matches your request. Compare products if you are unsure, because the steps that follow depend on that choice.
4. Click **Save and Continue**. The portal creates your application, records **Financing Type** from the product you chose, and opens the editor on the **next step your product requires**. The top bar lists the full workflow for **that** product, always starting with **Financing Type**.

Use **Save and Continue** to move forward and **Back** to fix an earlier screen.

## Draft applications and the Application list

**Application** in the sidebar is your list of financing requests. Drafts sit there together with submitted and other statuses. Open one to **resume** the editor and pick up where you stopped. While the application is still **Draft**, your progress is kept for the next visit.

## After Financing Type

Follow the progress bar. The middle steps are **only** what your product includes. Examples you might see (not every product has all of them) include **Company Details**, **Business & Guarantor Details**, **Financial Statements**, **Upload Supporting Documents**, **Provide Contract and Customer Details**, **Upload invoice(s)**, or a **Financing Structure** style question. Your product may use other labels or skip some of these entirely.

## When you see Financing Structure

Some products ask **How would you like to apply for financing?** with three choices. Use the one that matches your situation.

**Submit a new contract**  
*My invoice is under a contract that hasn't been approved by Cashsouk*  
For a **new** facility CashSouk has not approved yet. You will enter contract and customer details where the form asks. Invoice steps depend on the product.

**Use an existing contract**  
*My invoice is under a contract already approved by Cashsouk*  
When you already have an approved CashSouk contract. Choose it in **Select an existing contract**. If you see **No existing contracts**, pick another path or complete an approved facility first. Details may **prefill** from the contract you pick.

**Invoice-only financing**  
*I want to finance my invoice(s) without a contract*  
When the product supports invoice-led financing without a full new-contract path. You still complete customer or paymaster details where the product requires them.

## Declarations and submit

The last step is **Declarations**. Tick every box, then **Submit**. The application is no longer **Draft** and goes to the team for review. Check **Application** for the latest status.

If you get **Amendment requested**, fix what they flagged and **Resubmit**. When offers are ready, accept or sign **contract** and **invoice** offers as the product requires.
