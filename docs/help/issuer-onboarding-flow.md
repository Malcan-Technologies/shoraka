## Welcome

Issuer onboarding is the first step before you can submit financing applications on CashSouk. Issuers use the platform to raise financing against eligible invoices or contracts.

## Before You Start

There is a RM 150 onboarding fee before the issuer onboarding flow starts. This fee is separate from future financing application fees and note repayment activity.

You can onboard as:

- Individual account: for a single person account.
- Company account: for a business account with company information, directors, shareholders, and controllers where required.

## What You Will Do

During onboarding, you may be asked to:

- provide personal or company details,
- complete identity or business verification,
- provide director, shareholder, or controller details for company accounts,
- accept declarations and platform terms,
- provide supporting information requested by compliance checks.

## Review Outcome

After submission, your onboarding will be reviewed. The result can be:

- Approved: you can access issuer features and create financing applications.
- Follow-up required: you may need to provide updated or missing information.
- Rejected: your onboarding cannot proceed based on the review outcome.

## After Approval

Once approved, you can create a financing application. When you submit a financing application, a RM 50 application fee will be required. This fee will be implemented after the payment gateway is ready.

Submitting a financing application does not guarantee listing or funding. The application still goes through admin review, offer acceptance, and note preparation before it can become available to investors.
